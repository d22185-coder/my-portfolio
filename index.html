About Me

I’m a professional with interests in solar PV research, Solar cells and modules inspection through imaging (Electroluminescence (EL), Photoluminescence (PL), and Thermal Imaging), traveling, and writing. This website showcases my work, projects, and passions. I believe in creativity blended with professionalism. A hardcore renewable energy enthusiast specializing in solar energy systems (Pre & Post quality inspection of cells/modules) and an electrical engineer from the core. Learning methods to decrease CO2 levels in the environment and implementing them is my zeal. Looking for opportunities in the clean energy sector.

Projects

  • M. Tech Project – Life cycle analysis and determining the degradation and reliability issues in a 1MW solar power plant. The plant was beside our institute; we did a survey through I-V measurements, insulation testing, and SCADA data analysis to study module degradation and life cycle. Also examined extrinsic reliability issues: corrosion, moisture ingress, glass fracture, inverter reliability, and bypass diode failure.
  • M. Tech Project – Simulation of light induced degradation in CdTe thin film sources using SCAPS-1D.
  • M. Tech Project – Making Co2O3 nanoparticles by solution combustion method for supercapacitor applications.
  • M. Tech Project – Enhanced electrocatalyst activity of Co2O3 by copper metal interlayer for electrochemical water-splitting applications.
  • M. Tech Project – MATLAB Simulink model of hybrid cell balancing for battery management system (BMS) using switched resistor for passive BMS and switched capacitor for active BMS, with MOSFET switch for hybrid BMS.
  • B. Tech Project – Automatic source changer without interrupted power supply.
